## Content Summary

### Regulations
- **Ley 31814:** Complete analysis of Peru's AI Promotion Law (2023) - objectives, principles, scope, and implementation timeline
- **DS 115-2025-PCM:** Complete analysis of the AI Regulation including timelines, obligations, risk classification, prohibitions, and alignment with international standards

### Services

**Core Services:**
- **AI Readiness Assessment:** 2-week diagnostic with detailed methodology, deliverables, and pricing
- **Data Governance Implementation:** 6-month implementation with phased approach
- **Compliance Audit:** 3-week audit with formal report for regulators

**Specialized Services (DS 115-2025-PCM):**
- **AI Impact Assessment:** Mandatory evaluation for high-risk AI systems (Art. 30, 32) - 2-3 weeks per system
- **Data Quality Audit for AI:** Specialized audit for AI training data quality and bias prevention (Art. 6.e, 7.h) - 2 weeks
- **Algorithmic Bias Assessment:** Detection, measurement, and remediation of algorithmic bias (Art. 6.e, 6.c) - 2-4 weeks
- **Training & Capacity Building:** Comprehensive training programs for Data Owners, Stewards, and all staff (Art. 28.5) - Various durations

### Methodology

**Core Data Governance:**
- **Data Governance Framework:** Comprehensive framework covering conceptual foundations, organizational structure, operating models (centralized/decentralized/federated), best practices, and regulatory integration
- **Data Governance Fundamentals:** CDGP Body of Knowledge - 6 domains covering business drivers, ethics, privacy/security, stakeholder engagement, ROI measurement, and change management
- **Organizational Structures:** Frameworks comparison (DGI, DAMA-DMBOK2, Eckerson, DGPO), operating models, councils, roles, and operationalization strategies

**Data Stewardship:**
- **Data Stewardship:** Foundations of stewardship including roles (business/technical/project stewards), responsibilities, stewardship councils, building stewardship programs, and regulatory alignment

**Program & Project Management:**
- **Program Management:** Program vs function distinction, program components, establishing and managing programs, roadmaps, and PMI alignment
- **Implementation & Revitalization:** Designing new programs, revitalizing existing programs, sustainable adoption, change management, and compliance timelines

**Risk Management:**
- **Risk Management:** Risk categories (security, privacy, quality, compliance, AI), risk framework, DPIA for AI, controls, and regulatory alignment with DS 115-2025-PCM

**Data Quality & Metadata:**
- **Data Quality Framework:** Quality dimensions, governance integration, fit-for-purpose concept, implementation methodology, tools, and regulatory requirements
- **Metadata Management:** Technical/business/operational metadata, glossary, dictionary, catalog, lineage, and compliance requirements

**Tools & Policies:**
- **Data Governance Toolkit:** Tool categories, evaluation criteria, build vs. buy decisions, architecture options, and recommendations for Peruvian financial institutions
- **Policies & Standards:** Normative hierarchy, principle development, policy templates, standards development, compliance mechanisms

**Governance Value:**
- **Data Governance Initiatives:** How governance enables AI/ML projects, analytics, compliance, MDM, and digital transformation with ROI metrics

**Standards & Maturity:**
- **Maturity Model:** 1-5 scale with detailed criteria per dimension
- **ISO 42001:** AI Management Systems standard - structure, controls, certification
- **ISO 27002:** Security controls relevant for AI and data governance

### Industry
- **Banking:** Use cases, risks, quick wins, ROI considerations
- **Insurance:** Underwriting, claims, fraud detection specifics
- **Asset Management:** Model risk, valuations, regulatory requirements
- **Fintech:** Agile governance, limited resources, practical approach

### Case Studies
- **Scoring Crediticio:** Complete before/after with metrics and lessons learned
